What you'll do

Summary:  
The Lead Developer - Knowledge Graph & Semantics (f/m/d) designs, develops, and scales the Knowledge Graph platform that grounds AI use cases in trusted business context across SAP IT. Join a newly formed team working with cutting-edge graph technologies to define Knowledge Graph architecture, entity extraction pipelines, and hybrid RAG implementations for next-generation AI systems.

The Role:
- Design ontology-to-graph pipelines, collaborating with Ontologists and Data Scientists to translate domain knowledge into graph schemas
- Build entity extraction workflows to parse unstructured content to graph-aware structures
- Build context retrieval and delivery services that provide AI agents with rich, semantically meaningful information
- Implement efficient graph traversal algorithms and optimize query performance for large-scale enterprise data
- Integrate graph-based retrieval with vector search and implement SHACL validation pipelines
- Apply software engineering best practices including code reviews, testing, documentation, and CI/CD automation
- Provide technical guidance to team members and actively contribute to architectural and technical decision-making

What you bring

We are looking for an innovative, self-driven team player to become an integral part of our technical team:
- 5+ years of professional experience in software development with a focus on data-intensive applications
- Familiarity with semantic web standards (RDF, RDFS, SPARQL) and ontology concepts, or data science background with NLP/NER experience (Azure Cognitive Services, spaCy, entity extraction)
- Strong Python skills (PySpark, LangChain, Databricks APIs)
- Experience with at least one graph database (Neo4j, Neptune, Stardog, SAP HANA Graph is a plus) and query languages (Cypher, SPARQL, Gremlin)
- Good understanding of RAG architectures (vector databases, embeddings, reranking)
- Experience with Databricks, Delta Lake, or similar
- Good understanding of MLOps (MLflow, model serving, evaluation)
- Familiarity with agentic AI
- Proficient in both German and English, with strong communication skills
